How to install in Outlook:

1
Click Copy Signature above
2
Open Outlook → Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Email signature → New signature
3
Click inside the signature editor and press Ctrl+V (Windows) or Cmd+V (Mac) to paste
4
Give your signature a name and click Save
⚠️ If Copy Signature does not work: click inside the preview area, press Ctrl+A / Cmd+A to select all, then Ctrl+C / Cmd+C to copy manually, then paste into Outlook.
